What is Publishing?

Publishing is the process of sharing your game with others.
Usually, this is done using an installer, such as Click Once.
This can also be done by distributing an application file plus required dlls and the content folder.

Keep in mind that different computers have different hardware and software setups.
Your game may not run, or may run incorrectly, on some computers.
This may be due to a driver issue, a GPU issue, an OS issue, or some other hardware/software issue.
Without specific error information, it can be impossible to determine what's causing the problem.
